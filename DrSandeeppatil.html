Dr. Sandeep patil MBBS DGO DNB(Obst-Gynaecology) He has more than 12 years of experience in the field Obstetrics and Gynaecology. He has completed his MBBS from reputed Govt. Medical College, Miraj. After which he pursued his post-graduation from Govt. Medical College, Solapur. Further he studied his DNB masters at Southern Railway HQ hospital, Chennai.

He is skilled in High risk pregnancy care, treatment of complications in pregnancy like Diabetes or Hypertension in pregnancy, twin pregnancy, treatment of menstrual problems, PCOD, ovarian cysts, fibroid uterus. Shree Multispecialty Hospital is created to provide comprehensive care during all stages of pregnancy and delivery with 24x7 fully equipped labour room with Fetal-monitor(NST) & operation theatre. We have round the clock Gynaecologist and Neonatologist available for all type of obstetric emergencies with well equipped NICU.
